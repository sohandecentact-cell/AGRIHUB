Crop Details

Comprehensive information on different crops including varieties, cultivation methods, soil requirements, and harvesting techniques.

Crop Categories

Grains
Rice

Season: Monsoon | pH: 6.0-7.0 | Yield: 5-6 tons/acre

Requires well-drained soil, regular flooding during growing season. Three main varieties: Basmati, Jasmine, Parboiled.

Wheat

Season: Winter | pH: 6.0-7.5 | Yield: 4-5 tons/acre

Prefers cool climate. Harvested in spring. Tolerates various soil types. Requires 500-750mm rainfall.

Maize (Corn)

Season: Summer/Monsoon | pH: 5.5-7.5 | Yield: 6-8 tons/acre

Requires warm climate, well-drained fertile soil. High nutrient demand. Multiple cropping possible.

Fruits & Vegetables
Tomato

Season: Year-round | pH: 6.0-7.0 | Yield: 30-40 tons/acre

Needs warm weather, well-drained loamy soil. Support structure required. Harvest when fully ripe.

Cabbage

Season: Winter | pH: 6.0-7.5 | Yield: 30-35 tons/acre

Cool season crop. Rich soil with organic matter. Requires consistent moisture. Multiple cuttings possible.

Mango

Season: Summer | pH: 5.5-7.5 | Yield: 100-150 fruits/tree

Tropical/subtropical climate required. Takes 3-6 years to fruit. Well-drained soil essential.

Cash Crops
Cotton

Season: Summer | pH: 6.0-8.0 | Yield: 3-4 bales/acre

Requires warm climate, well-drained soil. High water requirement. Harvested 150-180 days after sowing.

Sugar Cane

Season: Year-round | pH: 6.0-7.0 | Yield: 40-50 tons/acre

Requires tropical climate, abundant water, rich soil. Long growth period (12-18 months).

Tea

Season: Perennial | pH: 4.5-5.5 | Yield: 2000-3000 kg/acre

Requires cool, hilly terrain with good rainfall. Well-drained acidic soil. 3-4 year wait before harvest.

Pulses
Chickpea

Season: Winter | pH: 6.0-7.5 | Yield: 2-2.5 tons/acre

Deep-rooted crop, suitable for dry regions. Improves soil nitrogen. Needs well-drained soil.

Lentil

Season: Winter | pH: 6.0-7.5 | Yield: 1.5-2 tons/acre

Cool season crop. Tolerates various soils. High protein content. Good in crop rotation.

Groundnut

Season: Summer | pH: 5.5-7.0 | Yield: 2-2.5 tons/acre

Requires warm climate. Sandy/loamy soil preferred. Develops pods underground.
